Immunizations
  • Adult (18-64)
    • Flu Shot
      • Due every calendar year
      • Will only display September - December
  • Adolescent (10-13)
    • DTaP or Td
      • 1 due between patient's 10th and 13th birthdays
    • Meningococcal
      • 1 due between patient's 11th and 13th birthdays
  • Child (0-2)
    • DTaP
      • 4 immunizations due by patient's 2nd birthday
    • Hepatitis A
      • 1 immunization due by patient's 2nd birthday
    • Hepatitis B
      • 3 immunizations due by patient's 2nd birthday
    • Hib
      • 3 immunizations due by patient's 2nd birthday
    • Influenza
      • 2 immunizations due by patient's 2nd birthday
    • MMR
      • 1 immunization due by patient's 2nd birthday
    • Pneumococcal
      • 4 immunizations due by patient's 2nd birthday
    • Polio
      • 3 immunizations due by patient's 2nd birthday
    • Rotavirus
      • 2 immunizations due by patient's 2nd birthday
    • Varicella
      • 1 immunization due by patient's 2nd birthday
Screenings
  • Mammogram (Women 50-74)
    • Mammogram
      • Due 3 years from previous screening, end of calendar year if never screened
      • Bilateral mastectomy exemption (MAMMO EXEMPT)
  • Cervical Cancer (Women 21-64)
    • Pap Smear
      • 21-30 - pap smear due 3 years from date of last screening
      • 31-64 - pap smear and HPV co-testing every 5 years
      • Hysterectomy exemption (HYSTERECTOMY)
  • Chlamydia Screening (16-24, prescribed birth control)
    • Chlamydia Screening
      • Due each calendar year
  • Colorectal Screening (50-75)
    • Colonoscopy
      • Due 10 years from last screening OR
    • Sigmoidoscopy
      • Due 5 years from last screening OR
    • Cologuard
      • Due 3 years from last screening OR
    • Fecal Occult Test
      • Due every calendar year
  • Depression (12+)
    • Depression Screening (PHQ-9)
      • Due each calendar year
  • Tobacco Use (18+)
    • Tobacco Screening
      • Due each calendar year
    • Cessation Counseling
      • If patient is current tobacco user, due each calendar year
Labs / Tests
  • Diuretics
    • BMP or Potassium or Creatinine Assay
      • Due each calendar year
  • Digoxin
    • Digoxin Assay
      • Due each calendar year
  • Antipsychotics (1-17)
    • Blood Glucose or LDL/HgbA1c
      • Due each calendar year
Disease Management
  • Diabetes
    • HgbA1c
      • Under 65: Reading below 8.0% due each calendar year
      • 65+: Reading below 9.0% due each calendar year
    • Kidneys - Nephropathy Screening / Care
      • Urine Protein Screening due each calendar year OR
      • ACE/ARB therapy (NOT CURRENTLY CHECKED BY QMAF)
    • Retinal Eye Exam (18-75)
      • Exam due every calendar year OR
      • Negative exam result in previous calendar year
  • Hypertension (18-85)
    • Blood Pressure
      • 18-59: under 139/89
      • 60-85 non-diabetic: under 149/89
      • 60-85 diabetic: under 139/79
  • COPD (42+)
    • Spirometry
      • Due each calendar year
  • Asthma (5+)
    • Prescribed Controller Medication
  • Depression
    • Prescribed antidepressant medication
  • Coronary Artery Disease / Ischemic Vascular Disease
    • Any age: No Aspirin or Antithrombotic Medication
    • Under 75: No Statin Medication
  • Rheumatoid Arthritis
    • No DMARD Medication
  • Bronchitis (18-64)
    • Prescribed Antibiotic
  • URI (3 mo - 18 yo)
    • Prescribed Antibiotic in first three days of URI dx
Additional Measures (Not in QMAF)
  • ACE Inhibitor / ARB
    • BMP
      • Due each calendar year
  • Acute Myocardial Infarction (AMI)
    • Beta Blocker treatment for 6 months after discharge
  • ADHD (6-12), prescribed new ADHD medication
    • One visit within 30 days of initial dispense
    • Two more visits within 10 months of medication dispense
  • Alcohol / Drug Episodde Treatment (13+)
    • One E/M visit within 14 days of onset
    • Two more visits within 30 days of onset
  • Beers List (65+)
    • Avoid "high risk" medications
  • Acute Myocardial Infarction (AMI)
    • BMP
      • Due each calendar year
  • Diabetes
    • Feet (18-75)
      • Visual inspection/sensory exam with mono filament and pulse exam due each calendar year
    • Statin Therapy (40-75)
      • Prescribed statin therapy
  • Inpatient Discharge Management
    • All discharged patients need to be seen in office within 7 days
    • Medication reconcilliation must be done
    • No readmission within 30 days of discharge
  • Primary Dx of Low Back Pain (18+)
    • Avoid all imaging studies within 28 days of dx
  • Obesity (3+)
    • Children (3-17)
      • Weight assessment adn nutrition / physical activity counseling each calendar year
    • Adults (18+)
      • Outside of normal range requires treatment plan (handout) each calendar year
      • 18-64: BMI of 18.5 - 24 considered normal
      • 65+: BMI of 23-29 considered normal
  • Osteoporosis (Women 67-85)
    • Must have DEXA or RX to treat osteoporosis in the 6 months following any fracture
  • Pharyngitis / Strep Throat / Acute Tonsillitis (2-18)
    • If dispensed an antibiotic must have had a strep test for the episode
  • Pregnancy
    • No elective delivery or induction less than 39 weeks
  • Well Care Visits
    • Children (0-6)
      • 0-15 months: 1-6 well care visits
      • 3-6 years: at least 1 annual visit
    • Adult
      • Med-advantage Insurance: annual visit
      • Commercial Insurance: at least one visit every 2 years
